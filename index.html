02 // Profile

The Person Behind The Code

Hi, I'm Mohammed. I’m a Computer Science student at AASTMT, which means I spend a lot of time staring at code. I speak the language of algorithms, APIs, and databases fluently.

But I noticed a frustrating problem in our field: Code is invisible. You can build the most robust architecture in the world, but to a client or stakeholder, it's just a black box. They can't see the logic.

That’s why I pivoted to Technical Motion Design. I realized I could use my engineering background to authentically visualize how software works. I don't just make things "look cool"—I translate your actual backend complexity into visuals that make people nod their heads and say, "Oh, I get it now."
